About

We are specialised in inclusive onboarding processes. Our main goal is increasing diversity at work place and creating mentorship programs which help companies embrace difference.

All of our hiring managers, interviewers and recruiters have been trained to understand and counteract their unconscious and conscious biases. Our hiring process and use techniques are fair (inclusive language in job ads, blind CV reviews, and standardized questions and scoring in interviews).

Our inclusive work ecosystem offers unbiased and respectful treatment, regardless of the environment. Inclusion celebrates the things that make all of us different, like age, gender, religion, education, etc. and how these differences offer empowerment and connection.
