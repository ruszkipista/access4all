Onboarding process

Diversity and Inclusiveness

We are specialised in inclusive onboarding processes. Our main goal is increasing diversity at work place and creating mentorship programs which help companies embrace difference. When companies administer and uphold the precepts of diversity within an organization, it creates a more enriched environment that fosters employee growth, innovation and productivity.

In parallel with diversity, an inclusive work ecosystem offers unbiased and respectful treatment, regardless of the environment. Inclusion celebrates the things that make all of us different, like age, gender, religion, education, etc. and how these differences offer empowerment and connection. Employees are treated fairly across the board and open to the same opportunities, tools and resources. With widely accepted social mores and office standards as its foundation, inclusion is the act of making an “outsider” feel accepted.

Onboarding procedure

  • Pre-onboarding - When the offer is accepted we will prepare everything for your first day and help you with all the necessary paperwork.
  • Welcome on board - We will keep it simple! On your first day you will meet all your co-workers, we will have team lunch and this will be a day for settling in. We will do everything to make you comfortable and adjust well. We will prepare timetable and agenda for you.
  • Role Training - We care about your development. You will be provided with a 30 day training. We will assign you a mentor for who will be there to help you with any questions you might have. All onboarding learning materials will be stored safely in a place that you can easily access and refer back to.
  • Easing transition to new role - After 30 days you will start transitioning to the new role, feel more comfortable and be able to start working with the team.

Performance measurement

We will set clear responsibilities, expactations and smart goals to help you visualize what success, quality, and productivity look like.
After 90 days we will conduct a performance review to recognize your efforts so far and help you improve.
